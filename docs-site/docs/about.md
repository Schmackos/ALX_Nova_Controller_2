# About ALX Nova

**Your Ears deserve the best!**

ALX Nova is an open-source audio ecosystem that lowers the threshold in the creation of audio controlling and producing devices. Achieved through a modular platform with standardised add-on mezzanine expansion modules, the platform can be used for prototyping as well as commercialised end-user devices.

## Platform Vision

The platform provides a standardised MCU with a Hardware Abstraction Layer (HAL) featuring hotpluggable, configurable high-level components like ADC, DSP, DAC and others, supporting specific SoCs from the likes of ESS Technology, Texas Instruments/Burr-Brown, Cirrus Logic and Analog Devices.

ALX Nova removes the need for complex proprietary tooling like SigmaStudio, eliminating vendor lock-in and limitations while facilitating mixing and matching SoCs to achieve the desired outcome. The platform enables the creation of custom-tailored audio devices, removing a high bar of entry and investment.

## Community & Sustainability

The platform brings a worldwide community of tinkerers and leading audio enthusiasts together, enabling them to join forces and embed expertise in a sustainable and reusable way (reducing e-waste). Community members create best-in-class expansion modules that work out of the box.

ALX Nova has the ambition to be the go-to choice when either using or developing audio products.

## Works With Certification

Community-created and certified **"Works With"** standardised add-on components can be sold through the ALX e-commerce store. ALX offers to offload the manufacturing and shipment process, or creators can handle this themselves. When sold through the ALX Store, the creator receives a percentage of the revenue.

"Works With" certification is available to both individual end-user oriented and larger-scale commercialised products, although with support restrictions due to its community nature.

## Certified Partners

Certified commercial partners are permitted to use the dedicated **"Certified"** badge, oriented towards larger-scale commercialised products with dedicated support. These can be sold through the ALX e-commerce store, through affiliate links listed on the ALX Certified devices section, or through other proprietary channels.

Only certified partners are permitted to use the dedicated "Works With" badge.

## What ALX Nova Provides

- **Hardware Abstraction Layer** for ADC, DSP, DACs and beyond
- Intelligent external amplifier controller (relay control) with smart auto-sensing
- WiFi and Ethernet network management
- REST API and WebSocket real-time control
- MQTT and Home Assistant integration
- OTA firmware updates with SHA256 verification
- Web configuration interface
- LVGL-based GUI on TFT display
- Push button and rotary encoder input
- Extensive documentation and community support
